Lieux visités
We will take the bus towards Highgate, moving from the urban cityscape of Kentish Town up the hill past ever more upmarket housing. The bowling green at the eastern edge of Hampstead Heath will make you feel like being in the English countryside, surrounded by trees and full of bird song.
